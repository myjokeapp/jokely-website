Request Account Deletion

We value your privacy. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (jokes, sets, recordings) at any time.

Option 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

This is the fastest way. Your data will be deleted immediately.

  1. Open Jokely App.
  2. Go to Profile / Settings.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion manually. We will process your request within 30 days.
