Callbacks & Themes

Callbacks are cheat codes.
What is a callback?
Theme
ü¶ä
A callback references an earlier joke to amplify the laugh. It makes the set feel like a finished story. Don't force them; plant seeds early.

ü¶ä
One or two solid callbacks is enough. Overusing them turns your show into an inside joke. Use them to reward attention.
